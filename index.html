About Us

Trusted Dental Hospital in Sainikpuri, Defence Colony & Malkajgiri – Hyderabad

Welcome to D&R Smiles Dental Hospital, your trusted destination for affordable, ethical, and expert dental care in Sainikpuri, Defence Colony, Hyderabad. We combine modern dental technology with compassionate care to deliver exceptional results for patients of all ages.

Located in the heart of Defence Colony, Sainikpuri, our clinic is designed to provide a calming ambience, supported by a team of experienced professionals committed to your oral health. Whether you're coming in for a regular check-up or a complex procedure, you can count on our supportive staff to make your visit stress-free and effective.
